## Ranking Rule

For each budget class:

1. Invalid submissions score 0 and are not ranked above valid submissions.
2. Valid submissions are ranked by landed payload `M_carry`.
3. Ties within 1 kg use the tie breakers in `benchmark/SCORING.md`.
